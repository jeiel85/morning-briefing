# Legal and Source Risk Checklist

This is not legal advice. Use this checklist before public launch and consult a qualified professional for commercial release.

## Source Use

- [ ] Does each source permit API/RSS use for this purpose?
- [ ] Are rate limits respected?
- [ ] Are attribution requirements met?
- [ ] Is full article text avoided unless licensed?
- [ ] Are paywalls respected?
- [ ] Are user comments/social data handled under platform terms?

## Copyright

- [ ] The app stores titles, URLs, metadata, and short snippets only.
- [ ] Generated summaries are short and source-linked.
- [ ] Original source links are prominent.
- [ ] The app does not replace reading the source article.
- [ ] The app avoids long quotations.

## Privacy

- [ ] Privacy policy explains collected data.
- [ ] Interest keywords are treated as personal data.
- [ ] Inactive window is treated as sensitive preference data.
- [ ] Account deletion is available.
- [ ] Email unsubscribe is available.
- [ ] Data retention periods are documented.

## AI Disclosure

- [ ] Users are told summaries are AI-assisted.
- [ ] Users are told to verify original sources for important decisions.
- [ ] Confidence labels are visible.
- [ ] Uncertainty is displayed.

## Email Compliance

- [ ] User explicitly opts into email.
- [ ] Sender identity is clear.
- [ ] Unsubscribe link exists.
- [ ] Email frequency is clear.
- [ ] No undisclosed tracking pixels.
